Venue Details

Hotel Prince Viraj is an environmentally conscious hotel that has heralded a new standard of environmental responsibility in the hospitality industry. The hotel has cool, open spaces that are perfectly complemented by its warm and intimate hospitality. It comprises of 37 decorated rooms and suites, 02 banquet halls, which can accommodate anywhere from 500 to 700 people, an unusual and stylish Lobby. Famous Multi-cuisine restaurant namely "Masala Mystery" as the name goes for lip smacking cuisines is a hit in the city serving Indian,Chinese,Tandoori and Continental fares. A 100 cover upscale restaurant serves daily Buffet Dinner --- Mocktail, Starters, Soup to Desserts, very resonably priced having both Vegetarian and Non-Vegetarian dishes as its spread. One needs to experience our service to understand our Hospitality. Seasoned International brand experienced staff are available in the Hotel to serve you. Be our guest and experience the best at Jabalpur.
